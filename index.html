Welcome to AskAcademia

   

A Student Help Management System for University Support

 
 
   

      AskAcademia is a platform where students can raise help requests related to academics, career guidance, or personal concerns. Faculty members can provide guidance and manage student queries. Alumni can also contribute by mentoring students with their valuable experiences.    

   

      Together, we build a supportive ecosystem for students, faculty, and alumni to connect and collaborate effectively.
